# 📅 Weekly Reviews

> Every Sunday. 30 minutes. Non-negotiable.
> Open this page. Be brutally honest. Then plan next week.

---

## How to Review

1. Open each tracker (DSA, LLD, HLD, AI, Speaking)
2. Check boxes for what you actually completed
3. Come back here and fill in the weekly reflection
4. Set 3 priorities for next week
5. If you skipped 3+ days on any track, write WHY and what you'll change
