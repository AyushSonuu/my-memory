## 🎯 The Phase Plan — Wake First, Sleep Follows

You can't force yourself to fall asleep. But you CAN set an alarm and drag yourself out.
After 2–3 days of forced early wake → body is exhausted → naturally sleeps earlier.

**Anchor = WAKE TIME (fixed). Sleep time adjusts naturally.**

```
Phase 1 (Week 1–2):  Wake 07:00 (alarm, no snooze) → body will push sleep to ~23:30
Phase 2 (Week 3–4):  Wake 06:30                     → sleep drifts to ~23:00
Phase 3 (Week 5–6):  Wake 06:00                     → sleep drifts to ~23:00
Phase 4 (Week 7+):   Wake 05:30 🎯                  → sleep at ~22:30
```

**Rules:**
- Wake time is NON-NEGOTIABLE. Same time every day, including weekends (±30 min on weekends OK).
- No naps longer than 20 min during crash zone (longer naps kill night sleep).
- First 3 days will suck. That's the price. By day 4–5, body adjusts.
- No caffeine after 14:00.
- Phone across the room at night — you HAVE to get up to turn off alarm.

## 📏 Rules

1. **Wake time is the anchor.** Set alarm, phone across room, NO SNOOZE. Sleep follows naturally.
2. **First 3 days will suck.** That's normal. By day 4–5, body adjusts and you'll feel sleepy by 23:00.
3. **Naps max 20 min** during crash zone. Longer naps destroy night sleep.
4. **No caffeine after 14:00.** Tea/coffee before 2 PM only.
5. **No screens after 22:45** — phone on silent, away from bed.
6. **If you miss a study block, DON'T sacrifice sleep to make up.** Carry forward.
7. **Track your actual sleep/wake times** — honesty drives progress.
8. **Move to next phase only when current phase is consistent for 2 weeks.**
9. **Phone across the room at bedtime.** You HAVE to physically get up to turn off alarm. This is the #1 hack.
