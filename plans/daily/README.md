## 📏 Rules

1. **Sleep at 23:00. No exceptions.** Everything else adjusts around this.
2. **DSA in the morning** — your brain is sharpest. Don't waste it on scrolling.
3. **The post-work crash (17:30–19:30) is PROTECTED REST** — not laziness, recovery.
4. **Evening study is 1.5 hrs max** on weekdays. Tired brain = wasted time.
5. **No screens after 22:45** — phone on silent, across the room.
6. **If you miss a block, don't try to "make up" by killing sleep.** Just carry forward.
7. **Weekend mornings are flexible** — sleep in if you need it. Sustainability > grind.
8. **Plan tomorrow before bed** — 5 min investment, massive next-day payoff.
