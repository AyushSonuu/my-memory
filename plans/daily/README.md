## 🎯 The Phase Plan — Don't Jump, Shift

You can't go from 8:00→5:30 wake overnight. That fails by day 3.

```
Phase 1 (Week 1–2):  Sleep 23:30 → Wake 07:00  (7.5 hrs sleep ✅)
Phase 2 (Week 3–4):  Sleep 23:00 → Wake 06:30  (7.5 hrs sleep ✅)
Phase 3 (Week 5+):   Sleep 23:00 → Wake 06:00  (7 hrs sleep ✅)
Phase 4 (Week 7+):   Sleep 22:30 → Wake 05:30  (7 hrs sleep ✅)
```

**Rule: Move sleep time FIRST, wake time follows.** You earn the early morning by sleeping earlier.

> Shift by 30 min every 2 weeks. Body adjusts. No alarms you can't keep.

## 📏 Rules

1. **Sleep on time is THE ONLY P0 in Phase 1.** Everything else adjusts.
2. **Move sleep time FIRST, wake time follows.** Never set an alarm you can't keep.
3. **The crash zone (17:30–19:30) is PROTECTED REST.** Always.
4. **No screens after 22:45** — phone on silent, across the room.
5. **If you miss a block, DON'T sacrifice sleep to make up.** Carry forward.
6. **Track your actual sleep/wake times** — honesty drives progress.
7. **Move to next phase only when current phase is consistent for 2 weeks.**
8. **Weekend = sleep in till 8:00.** Non-negotiable recovery.
