## 🧰 Frameworks (Quick Reference)

| Framework | When to Use | Structure |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| **PREP** | Meetings, standups, pitches | **P**oint → **R**eason → **E**xample → **P**oint |
| **3 Act** | Talks, presentations | **Hook** (30s) → **3 Points** → **Close** (callback) |
| **Feynman** | Explaining technical topics | Simple words → Analogy → If you can't simplify, you don't understand it |
